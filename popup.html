Enter = Search.
Ctrl + Shift + Backspace = Reset
Hold Ctrl to select multiple Filing Types.
There are three modes:
1. Company Page: Will Bring you to company search page. Doc in descending order
2. Linkgrabber: Will consolidate links from multiple pages and let you download documents. Specify cutoff year
3. Doc Download: Will let you download all documents. Note that downloading a large number of files will trigger CAPTCHA and your IP will be flagged. Use sparingly, and with VPN.
