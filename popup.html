Enter from any field to search.
Ctrl + Shift + Backspace = Reset
Hold Ctrl to select multiple Filing Types.
There are three modes:
1. Doc Page will Bring you to the company documents page and sort in descending order.
2. Linkgrabbe will consolidate links from multiple pages and let you download documents. Specify cutoff year
3. Downloader: Will let you download all documents. Note that downloading a large number of files will trigger CAPTCHA and your IP will be flagged. Use sparingly, and with VPN.
